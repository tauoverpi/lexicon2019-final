Base stat
===
health: 10(base)
stamina: health + strength(10 + 15 = 25(base))
defence: 0(base)
strength: health + 5(10 + 5 = 15(base))
morale: 50(base)

Mod effect
===
Dam(base): strength + stamina(15 + 25 = 40(base))
Dam(mod): enemy-health + enemy-stamina(10(base) + 25(base) = 35)
Dam(tot): (str + stm) - (eStr + Estm)
